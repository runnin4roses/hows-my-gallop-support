# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 2026-06-22*

How's My Gallop is designed to keep your data on your devices.

## What the app collects

- **Location (GPS):** Used to calculate your live speed and to record the route
  of your ride.
- **Workout data:** The app writes a workout (type: Equestrian Sports) to Apple
  Health when you finish a ride, with the GPS route attached. It reads heart
  rate and active energy from Apple Health during the workout so they can be
  recorded with the ride.

## What the app does *not* do

- **No data leaves your device or your iCloud Health data.** How's My Gallop has
  no servers and makes no network requests. Your location and workout data are
  not transmitted to the developer or any third party.
- **No analytics or tracking.** No third-party SDKs, no crash reporters, no ad
  networks.
- **No account.** You don't sign in. There's no user identity to associate data
  with.

## Where your data is stored

- GPS samples used for live speed are processed in-memory on your Apple Watch
  and discarded after the ride.
- The saved workout and its route live in Apple Health, on your iPhone and
  Apple Watch, governed by Apple's privacy controls. You can delete any
  individual workout from the Health app at any time.

## Permissions the app requests

- **Location — While Using:** required to measure your speed and record the
  route.
- **HealthKit (Read & Write):** required to save the workout and route, and to
  include heart rate / energy data alongside the workout.

You can revoke either permission in iPhone Settings at any time. The app will
stop tracking if you do.
